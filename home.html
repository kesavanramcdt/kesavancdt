K7's Summer Internship at the CDT

Hi my name is Kesavan Ramakrishnan and I'm a rising senior at Vista Del Lago High School in Folsom, CA. I was able to get the opportunity to work with CDT through the CTE Program that our school district holds. Throughout my one month here, I've met countless groups and gained a lot of experience of how it is to work in a professional setting. I've also gained technical skills and explored different tools such as leaflet, ARCGIS, etc. On this website theres links at the top to different maps and charts I created so please check them out!

What I've done

  • Learned Leaflet, GIS Tools, and became familiar with Azure, Snowflake, and other industrial tools
  • Met many different ODS Teams including the Web Dev, Data, MMBI, GIS, and UX teams
  • Attended a ODS full in-person team meeting, Statewide GIS Community of Practice Monthly meeting, etc
  • Toured the high-security data center
